Established in Lahore, Pakistan, LA is a innovative yet very flexible Automation Solutions provider and integrator for Commercial and Industrial facilities in Pakistan & Gulf Countries.

We offer customized integrated automation solutions. Also known as 'Industrial Automation' or 'Control & Automation', we are experts in the installation of advanced high-performance technical systems to automate tasks that would normally be carried out by human. Specialists in the integration of the finest building automation, building control and low current systems, our bespoke technical solutions can control all aspects of the building's functionality at the touch of a screen - from temperature control to production control.

We provide customized control panels for Pump Motor, Central Air Conditioning System, Fire Alarm Systems, Diesel Generators, Automatic Transfer Switches, Ultrasonic Liquid Level Senors, Motorized Valves, Hot/Cold Temp Sensor/Transducer, Water Pressure Control Systems. As well as offering greater reliability and convenience, solutions increase energy efficiency, whilst reducing energy and maintenance costs.
